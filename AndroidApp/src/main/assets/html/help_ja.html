Flag Snag is a rapid flag matching game in which you have to match the maximum possible flags before the timer runs down to zero. There will be four flags with four options displayed. You have to drag and drop the flag name on the flag. As soon as you match the name against the correct flag the next four flags will be displayed.

カテゴリ: 世界フラッグス
カテゴリ: 米国フラッグス

カテゴリ: 世界フラッグス

When you select "World Flags" category, flags of the countries of the world will be displayed. There are 194 flags of sovereign countries of the world in this category. You have 3 minutes to match as many flags as you can.

カテゴリ: 米国フラッグス

When you select "US Flags" category, flags of the states of United States and its dependencies will be displayed. There are 65 flags in this category. This category also includes flags of United States armed forces. You have 1 minute to match as many flags as you can.

カテゴリ: 英国のフラッグス

When you select "UK Flags" category, flags of the counties of United Kingdom and its dependencies will be displayed. There are 66 flags in this cateogory. This category also includes flags of Royal armed forces. You have 1 minute to match as many flags as you can.
